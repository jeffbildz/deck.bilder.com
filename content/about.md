I'm Jeff Bilder — a cloud and infrastructure leader with **25 years** in IT, spanning the
software, risk management, and security sectors.

My career started where a lot of good infrastructure people start: hands-on, configuring
redundant Cisco networks, building Linux mail clusters, and writing scripts to make manual
work disappear. Over time that grew into leading teams, architecting datacenters, managing
multi-million-dollar budgets, and ultimately automating multi-region AWS estates across the
US, EU, and GovCloud.

The throughline has never changed: **take systems that are sprawling, fragile, and scary to
touch, and turn them into platforms that are automated, observable, and secure by default.**

Today I lead IT Operations and DevOps at [Origami Risk](https://www.origamirisk.com), where
I've converted our infrastructure to Terraform-managed IaC, containerized the full SaaS
application stack, built CI/CD pipelines, and shipped DISA STIG-compliant imaging — all
peer-reviewed in Git.

## What I care about

- **Automation over heroics.** If a deploy needs someone who "knows the trick," it's broken.
- **Security as a default, not a gate.** STIG-hardened, audit-ready, RBAC-governed — by design.
- **Clarity.** The best systems explain themselves. So should the people who run them — which
  is why I write.

## Strengths

Strong technical leadership with deep hands-on experience: AWS at scale, Terraform/Ansible
automation, containerization, observability, and a security-first mindset built on a networking
and intrusion-detection foundation. Equally comfortable in a terminal or in front of executives
making sure goals are understood and met.
