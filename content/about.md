I'm Jeff Bilder — a cloud architect and operations leader who builds the boring,
dependable infrastructure that lets teams move fast without breaking things.

Over the years I've worn the hats of **cloud architect**, **DevOps manager**,
**infrastructure manager**, and **security operations manager** — and the thread
through all of them is the same: take systems that are sprawling, fragile, and
scary to touch, and turn them into platforms that are automated, observable, and
secure by default.

## What I care about

- **Reproducibility over heroics.** If a deploy needs a person who "knows the trick,"
  it's broken. Git should be the source of truth.
- **Security as a default, not a gate.** The secure path should also be the easy path.
- **Clarity.** The best systems explain themselves. So should the people who run them —
  which is why I write.
